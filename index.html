Flexible living spaces within compact apartment

The project was carried out for a client who wanted maximum flexibility built into the scheme, so that the tiny apartment could be moved around to suit the needs of its tenants. The flat was also extended to incorporate an additional seven square-metres, while a perforated concrete brick wall was built to create a sleeping niche for the bed.

simple and inexpensive

Most of the furniture was custom-made due to the small dimensions of the property. The architects chose colours that would enhance the feeling of space, which they used in combination with earthy tones to create a cosier environment.
